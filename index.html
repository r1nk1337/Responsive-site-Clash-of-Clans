BARBARIAN

The Barbarian is 1 of 6 troops in the Home Village that originated from Clash of Clans and appears in Clash Mini, the others being Archer, Wizard, P.E.K.K.A, Valkyrie, and Bowler. Barbarians along with Archers, Miners, and Hog Riders are the only 4 troops not based on fiction or legend/myth.
